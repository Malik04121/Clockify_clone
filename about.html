Our story

Clockify was officially launched in 2017. Back then, we were charging clients by the hour, so we needed to know how much time we were spending on projects and our whole team needed to track time. Nothing fancy or intrusive. We just needed a record of how much time a certain project took in a certain month, and how much we need to bill clients.

One day, we got an email from the company whose time tracker we were using. The email was notifying us that, starting next month, our monthly bill would increase from $500 to $1,000. Even before that we felt that we were overpaying the tool, and this new price was simply too much. At that moment, we decided to look for an alternative.

We started searching for another solution, but all the time tracking apps on the market were either too complicated or too expensive - especially for bigger teams, like ours.

So, we decided to create our own time tracker and offer it to anyone for absolutely free. Why? We know that each dollar counts when you're a startup and we believe every team should be able to track time without having to pay a fortune.

In the meantime, a lot of people started using Clockify and we started receiving a ton of feature requests. So, we've decided to invest further into Clockify's development and completely focus on building products. We've added a ton of extra features, and started charging for them to teams that needed more.

How we keep Clockify free

The #1 question we receive all the time is: "How do you make money if the service is free? What's the catch?"

Short answer: we have paid plans that have additional features aimed towards professionals.

When something good is offered for free, like Clockify, it usually comes with a hidden price - most usually it's privacy, something tech industry is notorious for. That's not the case with Clockify.

We never sell or share any data with anyone. Why? Because we don't have to. We don't have investors or shareholders hungry for profit, so we don't face pressure to make a quick buck by any means necessary. We are completely independent and free to always do the right thing.

How can we afford this? First, we make Clockify accessible to everyone, and then we provide even more value to those on a paid subscription:

  • The basic version is free, so anyone can easily get started. This lets us make time tracking accessible to everyone.
  • Some teams decide to upgrade to a paid plan so they can access extra features, like scheduling and time off tracking.

This structure lets us democratize time tracking for everyone, without ever needing to make a profit at a cost of someone's privacy. We hope you enjoy using Clockify as much as we enjoy developing it. If you have any questions or feedback, send us an email - we always love to hear from our users!
